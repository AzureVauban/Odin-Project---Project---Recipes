Ingredients

    0.75 lb Cooked Turkey Meant 2 stalks of Celery 2 Green Onions 0.50 lb Red Bell Pepper 3 Tbsp Mayo 2 Tbsp Dijon-style Mustard 1 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar 1 Tbsp White Sugar 0.25 Tbsp Salt
Note: the listed ingredients were made with the intention of 24 servings

Instructions

    1. Pulse the turkey and Vegetables into a food processor until finely chopped. 2. Transfer to a bowl, then mix in the remaining ingredients. 3. Cover and chill in fridge for 8 hours and allow the flavors to meld.

Storing Instructions

It is best to store the turkey salad in an airtight container in a refrigerator. When properly preapred, it should last from 3 to 5 days. Recipe was sourced from this amazing gobble gobbler
