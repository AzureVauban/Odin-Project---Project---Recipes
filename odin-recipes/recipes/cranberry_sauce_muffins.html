Quick Info

    Prep Time: 15 min Servings: 18 Cook Time: 20 minutes ()Total cook time is 35 minutes)

Ingredients

    2 cups all-puepose flour 0.50 cup packed brown sugar 0.25 cup white sugar 1 Tbsp baking powder 0.50 Tsp ground cinnamon 0.50 Tsp ground cardamom 0.50 Tsp salt 1 cup Cranberry sauce (Peferably leftover Cranberry sauce) 0.75 cup milk (Peferably whole milk) 0.25 cup vegetable oil 1 large egg (slightly beaten) 1 Tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

    1. Gather all ingredients. 2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fabrenheit or 200 degrees celisus. Line 18 muffin cups with paper liners. 3. Beat Cranberry sauce, milk, oil, egg, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until well combined. 4. Whisk flour, brown sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, cardamom, and salt together in a seperate bowl. 5. Stir dry ingredients into wet ingredients until batter is just moistened. 6. Pour into the prepared muffin cups. 7. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
